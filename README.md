# ⚔️ The Last Stand

A 2D game where an **ancient warrior comes out of retirement** to face a monster threatening his village — because some battles can't be left to others.

---

## 📖 Story

Once a legendary fighter, he chose a quiet life far from the battlefield. But when a fearsome monster begins terrorizing his village, the old warrior straps on his armor one final time. Peace will have to wait.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Take control of the ancient warrior and face the monster that threatens everything he holds dear. Use your skills and experience from a lifetime of battles to protect your village in this last stand.
